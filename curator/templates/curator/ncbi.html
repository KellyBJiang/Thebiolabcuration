Title Chronic Rosiglitazone Mouse Aorta
Organism Mus musculus
Experiment type Expression profiling by array
Summary To better understand what genes are regulated by PPAR gamma, an experimental dataset was generated by microarray analysis, in duplicate, of pooled aortic mRNA isolated from adult male mice (C57BL/6J strain) treated for 21 days with a PPAR gamma agonist (rosiglitazone) or vehicle.
